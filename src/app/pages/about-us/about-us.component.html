About Us

Primary care respiratory group & primary care diabetic group

organisations consists of primary care physicians of Sri Lanka who have special interest and vast exposure in this both medical disciplines , now joined hands together to learn and educate peers in research and new developments in respiratory and diabetes management at primary care level in Sri Lanka.

Vision

Our vision is of a global population breathing and feeling well through universal access to right care.

Mission

Our mission is to work locally in primary care and collaborate globally to improve respiratory health and diabetics.

Scope

Our scope is research and education on improved primary prevention, recognition, diagnosis, treatment and palliation of non- communicable respiratory and diabetic diseases.
