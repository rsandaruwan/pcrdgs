Primary Care Respiratory Group (PCRG)

The Primary Care Respiratory Group – Sri Lanka (PCRGSL) is a professional association comprised primarily of Primary Care Physicians with a special interest in the treatment of respiratory system-related disorders in the community. PCRGSL is a full member of the International Primary Care Respiratory Group (IPCRG), a globally recognized organization in this field (www.theiperg.org). Since its start, PCRGSL has held annual respiratory conferences as well as provincial CME sessions in respiratory medicine (2006). In 2017, we had a very successful international scientific conference with over 300 delegates from all around the world.
