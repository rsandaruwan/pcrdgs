Eligibility Criteria

Doctors fully or provisionally registered in Sri Lanka Medical Council (SLMC). (PCRG council has the final discretion on selection of participants for the course).

Duration

The total duration for this course is 08 months ( one module per month). It will be conducted every third Sunday from 9.0 a.m to 1.00 p.m. (4 hours of duration)

Evaluation and Certification Criteria

Attendance : Participants should attend at least 7 modules, including the pre - test and the post - test.
Award of the final certification : Compulsary attendance with 50% marks on the exam.
