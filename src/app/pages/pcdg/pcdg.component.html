Primary Care Diabetic Group (PCDG)

The Primary Care diabetic Group – Sri Lanka (PCRGSL) is a professional association comprised primarily of Primary Care Physicians with a special interest in the treatment of diabetic-related disorders in the community. Just like the primary care respiratory group we are expanding the reach into the community. We have not yet started to course to get the practitioners more experienced in the feild but we aim to.

Diabetes in Sri lanka

According to local statistics, the prevalence of diabetes mellitus is on the rise. In 1990, the earliest study on a rural community found a frequency of 2.5 percent. In 2005, the largest-ever study on the prevalence of diabetes in Sri Lanka was published.

Diabetes mellitus, formerly thought to be a disease of the rich world, has evolved into a global pandemic, with two-thirds of the world’s diabetic population living in poor countries. Males had a prevalence of 14.2%, while females had a prevalence of 13.5 %
